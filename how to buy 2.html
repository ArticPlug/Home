Available Payment Methods

PayPal

Fast, secure, and widely accepted. We accept both PayPal balance and credit card payments.

  1. Log in to PayPal.
  2. Select Send Money → Friends & Family.
  3. Enter our PayPal email + the exact amount.
  4. In the message/note field, write: What you’re buying, Your email, Full shipping address (Country, ZIP, Street, City)
  5. Confirm and send.

Crypto

Secure payment processing with encryption for your peace of mind.

  1. Enter your card details at checkout
  2. Provide billing information
  3. Complete 3D Secure verification if required
  4. Receive instant payment confirmation

Bank Transfer

Direct bank transfer for those who prefer traditional payment methods.

  1. Select bank transfer at checkout
  2. We'll provide our banking details
  3. Complete the transfer from your bank
  4. Send us the payment confirmation
  5. We'll ship your order once payment clears
