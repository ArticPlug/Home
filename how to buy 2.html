Available Payment Methods

PayPal

Fast, secure, and widely accepted. We accept both PayPal balance and credit card payments.

  1. Select PayPal at checkout
  2. Log in to your PayPal account
  3. Review your order details
  4. Confirm the payment
  5. You'll receive an order confirmation email

Credit/Debit Card

Secure payment processing with encryption for your peace of mind.

  1. Enter your card details at checkout
  2. Provide billing information
  3. Complete 3D Secure verification if required
  4. Receive instant payment confirmation

Bank Transfer

Direct bank transfer for those who prefer traditional payment methods.

  1. Select bank transfer at checkout
  2. We'll provide our banking details
  3. Complete the transfer from your bank
  4. Send us the payment confirmation
  5. We'll ship your order once payment clears

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We typically process orders within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days, while express shipping (available at checkout) delivers in 1-3 business days. International shipping times vary by destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for items in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or we made an error in your order.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

How do I know what size to order?

We provide detailed size charts for each product. If you're unsure, contact our customer service team who can help you select the right size based on your measurements and fit preference.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely. We are an authorized retailer of Canada Goose, Moose Knuckles, and Moncler products. Every item comes with authenticity guarantees and original manufacturer warranties.
