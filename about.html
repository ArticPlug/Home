Frequently Asked Questions

How can I verify the authenticity of your products?

All our products come with authenticity certificates and original manufacturer tags. We are authorized retailers for Canada Goose, Moncler, and Moose Knuckles, and each item includes the official warranty documentation.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for items in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or we made an error in your order.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

How do I know what size to order?

We provide detailed size charts for each product. If you're unsure, contact our customer service team who can help you select the right size based on your measurements and fit preference.
